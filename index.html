What we do

We are good in creating sites for individuals and small businesses. Your website is the first impression your customers will get, so make sure it’s a good one. We are not using templates and write every part of your website ourselves. The process usually starts with a design in case you don't have one. If you already have a design - we can work with it.

Simplicity is one of the golden rules of website design. People should have an enjoyable, positive experience when using your website. Our approach is to create a website that strengthens your company’s brand while ensuring ease of use and simplicity for your audience.

Digital design concept is created using your guidelines for a personalised look and feel. Feedback on the design concept is essential to give you the opportunity to express your thoughts on the design and make any changes where needed before the final version.

We are keen advocates of producing prototypes before fully coding. These prototypes are a hybrid of design and interaction that enable you to experience how a user interface will feel before it is coded. This gives an opportunity to provide feedback at an early stage.

Once the design has been finalised, the creation of the website begins. The website development process starts by taking the graphical elements (colors, fonts, layout, images) defined in the design process. Then we use HTML, CSS, JavaScript (and React if needed) to produce responsive websites and web apps that provide users the best and most appropriate experience suited to their device and browser.
